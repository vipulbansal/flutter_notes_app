Privacy Policy for Flutter Notes App

Last Updated: April 13, 2025

Thank you for choosing to use our Flutter Notes App. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our application. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to ensuring that your personal information remains secure.

Information Collection and Use

Flutter Notes App does not collect any personal information. The app is designed to function entirely offline and locally on your device.

All notes and content you create in the app are stored exclusively on your device using Hive, a local database solution. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

Data Storage

All data created and managed in the app (including your notes, preferences, and app settings) is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to, nor do we maintain any copies of your data on external servers.

Automatic Data Collection

Analytics and Crash Reporting: The app does not include any analytics or crash reporting tools that would send data to external servers.

Device Permissions: The app only requires minimal standard permissions needed to function properly:

  • Storage access (to save your notes locally)

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Since we do not collect any personal information or user data, we do not share any information with third parties. The content of your notes remains private and is never transmitted outside of your device.

Data Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of your data. The Flutter Notes App uses industry-standard security measures for local data storage. However, please be aware that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

For additional security, we recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Using a device password or biometric authentication
  • Not sharing your device with unauthorized users

Children's Privacy

Our app does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. Since our app does not collect personal information from any users, this includes children under the age of 13.
